Spelling homework

What's it all about.

  • Create spellings homework for your students to complete.
  • Enter whatever words you want, in English or any other language, and how you want them to be understood.
  • The site will SPEAK the words and their definitions, for the pupil to spell.
  • Pupils can submit their work for teachers to review.
  • Schools and colleges can fully self-serve.
  • Pre-built assignments based on standard curriculum expectations.
